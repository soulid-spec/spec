# RFC-SOULID-0002

## SOUL ID v0.2 — Ownership, Signatures and Trust Layer

Status: Draft  
Category: Standards Track  
Author: Metrono.ai  
Date: 2026-03-31  

---

## Abstract

This document extends SOUL ID v0.1 by introducing ownership, cryptographic signatures, and a trust layer.

These additions enable verifiable identity, provenance, and trust between entities and runtimes.

---

## 1. Introduction

SOUL ID v0.1 defines identity and structure.

However, identity alone is insufficient without:

- ownership  
- authenticity  
- trust  

SOUL ID v0.2 introduces mechanisms to:

- verify entity origin  
- establish trust between parties  
- enable secure interoperability  

---

## 2. New Concepts

### 2.1 Owner

The entity or authority that controls and defines a SOUL ID.

### 2.2 Signature

A cryptographic proof that a Soul Document has been issued or approved by its owner.

### 2.3 Trust Layer

A mechanism to evaluate the validity and reputation of entities.
