# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in the SOUL ID project a welcoming experience for everyone, regardless of background, experience level, or identity.

## Our Standards

**Expected behavior:**

- Be respectful and constructive in discussions
- Assume good faith from other contributors
- Focus on ideas, not individuals
- Accept feedback gracefully

**Unacceptable behavior:**

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Deliberately hostile or disruptive contributions
- Publishing others' private information without consent

## Enforcement

Violations may be reported to **conduct@soulid.io**.

Maintainers reserve the right to remove, edit, or reject contributions that do not align with this Code of Conduct, and to ban contributors who engage in harmful behavior.
